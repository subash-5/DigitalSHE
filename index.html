About us

Project Digital SHE

This program is in support of Ministry of Social Development, Bagmati Province Government for the young girls studying +2, Engineering and IT. We are conducting 5 days training program on Robot Making, Hardware Programming, 3D Printing and Drone Making at Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur, Dolakha, Hetauda from 27th Jestha to Jestha 31 and Ashar 1 till Ashar 5. If you are from these Municipality, do register yourself through this form: shorturl.at/vzFR2 We are starting this project from Emerging Technology Knowledge transfer further backed by overall Digital Domain knowledge transfer in partnership with Digital Technologies leaders from all around the country. A portal is around the corner to visualize the status quo of women in digital space. Let's strengthen Digital Equality together.
